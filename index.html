Who We Are

Built for the viewer who wants more than the algorithm.

Magis TV Prime is an app development project dedicated to helping people decide what to watch — without the scroll fatigue. We build tools that catalogue, compare, and gently recommend titles across the entire landscape of modern entertainment, from prestige drama to Saturday-night popcorn.

As a product of AZTEC ONLINE LIMITED, we operate with the highest standards of professionalism, legal compliance, and transparency. Our mission is to deliver a thoughtful, well-crafted companion that respects your time and sharpens your taste.

We believe a recommendation is a small act of care. Every feature, every list, every suggestion inside Magis TV Prime is designed to make your next hour of viewing feel chosen rather than settled for.
